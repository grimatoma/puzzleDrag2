This is the recipe that bakes [[resources:bread]] — the farm economy's first real payoff. It consumes [[resources:flour]] and [[resources:eggs]] at the [[buildings:bakery]], turning two raw farm outputs into a sellable, packable good.

It sits at the end of a chain that starts on the board: wheat tiles credit flour, bird tiles credit eggs, and the Bakery combines them. Building the [[buildings:mill]] first lowers the flour this recipe needs by one, tightening the loop. Because bread doubles as expedition food, this recipe is effectively how a farm pays for a mine run.
