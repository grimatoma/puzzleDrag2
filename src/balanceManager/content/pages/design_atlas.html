The required spine

Seven towns the finale needs, in the order the map suggests. Each adds at most one new board, one crux, one win task, and a signature building that exports a bonus to every other town once complete.

Town 1 · home — Hearthwood Vale  Farm

Environment. A gentle forgotten vale around a cold hearth ([[zones:home]]). Radial growth — the settlement spreads outward from the evolving hearth-landmark as wilderness recedes.

Crux. The tutorial home. Teaches every verb — [[systems:chaining|chain]], refine, fill orders, raise a [[buildings:housing|House]] and hire a worker, tier up — with no pressure. Your home forever.

Hazard · boss. None. A safe teacher.

Win · signature. Save coins and build the Caravan · the Caravan Yard (the means to found every other town).

Special. Climbs on farm goods alone (no coins, no mine goods). Landmark: cold hearth → village green → market cross → town square.

Town 2 — Two Rivers  Farm Mine

Environment. A calm valley where two forks meet. Twin-fork growth — the town fills the land between the rivers, a mill on each bank.

Crux. The combination. The first mine, deliberately hazard-free: you learn to pack farm food into expedition turns and bring back ore & metal. The quiet "aha" of the whole economy.

Hazard · boss. None (calm teacher).

Win · signature. Fill a grand order demanding both farm & mine goods · the Quartermaster's Hall (packs food into rations more efficiently, everywhere).

Founding · gate. A resource caravan (12 [[resources:plank|plank]] · 10 [[resources:bread|bread]] · 8 [[resources:hay_bundle|hay]] — farm goods, not coins); home ≥ Hamlet + own a farm.

Ladder (proposed, 3 tiers): Camp (4) → Diggings (7) → Boomtown (10). Tier-ups demand brick/iron the mine itself digs. tuning-locked baseline

Town 3 — Cinderhold  Farm Mine

Environment. Glowing lava fields cooling into obsidian terraces. Switchback-terrace growth — hairpin roads climb the slope, each rung claiming a higher cooled shelf.

Crux. Mine-hazard management. A rising heat meter channels your routes; the frontier metal obsidian is only won at the dangerous edge.

Hazard · boss. Heat tiles, ash, ember-rain, tremors · [[bosses:ember_drake]] — heat pools on the lowest terrace first; anything left two turns burns. Land 3 iron bars before it floods.

Win · signature. Best the capstone boss · the Pit Canteen (+ration-turns on any mine expedition, everywhere).

Special. New resource obsidian (frontier board metal). Landmark: brazier → tap-furnace → great forge → Magma Crown.

Ladder (proposed, 4 tiers): Ashfall Camp (3) → Emberforge Terrace (6) → Cinderhold (10) → Magma Crown (14); scales on block + obsidian + [[resources:gold_bar|gold]] + coins.

Town 4 — Gullcliff  Farm Mine Fish

Environment. A bare sea-cliff carved into railed terraces and harbour shelves. Vertical-cliff growth — shelves linked by stairs and a cargo lift; the harbour floods and drains with the tide. (cf. coded [[zones:harbor]], Saltspray.)

Crux. The full stack. The first three-board town: farm bait + mine tackle fund the fishery. Vertical logistics — where you mine (low) and craft (high) sit on different levels.

Hazard · boss. Squall, riptide, rogue wave, gull raids · [[bosses:storm]] — demands 4+ fish chains; the lighthouse sweep briefly calms one column per pass. Land 6 fillets before the window closes.

Win · signature. The Regatta · the Bait Shack (a standing fishing bonus, everywhere).

Special. Board resource [[resources:pearls]] (oyster tiles). Landmark: tide-watch post → stone beacon → [[buildings:lighthouse|lighthouse]] → Beaconhead.

Ladder (proposed, 4 tiers): Tidewatch Landing (3) → Cliffstair Hamlet (6) → Gullcliff Town (10) → Beaconhead (15); scales on [[resources:plank|plank]] + [[resources:pearls|pearls]] + coins.

Town 5 — Greenmeadow  Farm Mine

Environment. A rich, broad meadow ([[zones:meadow]]) — the most generous farmland yet. Radial growth around a great barn.

Crux. Farm hazard at scale. The richest yields in the game, beset by [[hazards:rats|rats]]: a spreading pest you must manage while harvesting hard.

Hazard · boss. Rat infestation · [[bosses:mossback]] (lent from Thornwild) — hides tiles under moss; a roaming sun-shaft reveals them. Gather the harvest before the meadow swallows it.

Win · signature. Raise a great building · the Cattery (rat / pest management, everywhere).

Special. Landmark: meadow camp → greenfield → meadowgate → great barn.

Town 6 — Glimmerdeep  Mine only

Environment. A chain of unlit caverns opened one by one (cf. coded [[zones:caves]], Lanternlit Caves). Linked-chambers growth — each rung lights and clears the next chamber; cart-rails ferry resources between them. Light is life: unlit tiles can't be chained until a lantern reaches them.

Crux. Provision under scarcity. The deliberate exception: a mine with no farm. You must import food from your other towns to keep the expeditions running — a logistics test, not a farming one.

Hazard · boss. [[hazards:cave_in|Cave-in]], [[hazards:gas_vent|gas vent]], sinkhole · [[bosses:old_stoneface]] — drops rubble and snuffs the nearest lantern; keep the chamber lit while clearing 20 stone tiles.

Win · signature. Fill the great shipment under scarcity · the Supply Depot (an idle resource drip, everywhere — the most coveted cross-zone unlock).

Special. Board resource crystal (+ coded [[resources:cut_gem|cut gem]]). Landmark: lantern post → crystal cairn → geode shrine → resonant spire → the Heart Geode.

Town 7 · finale — Goldgrass · the Golden Capital  Farm Mine Fish

Environment. Golden prairie urbanised into grand civic districts, ringed by permanent fields (cf. coded [[zones:oldcapital]]). Axial-boulevard growth — a monumental central avenue and ring road; the widest layout in the game.

Crux. Everything at once. All three boards at the grandest scale; the capstone of scale and prestige.

Hazard · boss. Locust, [[hazards:fire|wildfire]], drought, stampede · Goldhorn (proposed) — a plains-bull that charges every few turns and tramples a full band; herd the right resource into the stockpens before it flattens them.

Win · signature. Build the Monument — the finale (see Progression). Gate: all required-spine towns complete.

Special. Landmark: waystone cross → market cross → civic fountain → grand plaza → the Golden Monument.

Ladder (proposed, 5 tiers): Crossroads Waystation (4) → Granary Town (8) → Market City (14) → Goldgrass Reach (20) → The Golden Capital (26); scales on [[resources:flour|flour]] + [[resources:plank|plank]] + [[resources:gold_bar|gold]] + coins.

The optional frontier

Five further sites you may found on reach + caravan. None is required for the finale; each ships its own hazard, boss, tier ladder, and a cross-zone export worth the detour.

Mirefen Hollow  Fish

Environment. Black-water fen and reed beds planked into buildable hummocks ([[zones:mirefen]]). Ribbon-stilt boardwalk — the town spreads island to island.

Crux · special. A high/low tide cycle: harvest at high tide, stranded at low. Tide-reading is the skill. New resource fenmead (brewed, gates the top rung).

Hazard · boss. Tidewater flood, miasma fog, leech bloom, will-o'-wisp · [[bosses:quagmire]].

Export. A tide-reading idle drip. Landmark: reed shrine → stilt fountain → bog-lantern beacon → the Heron Gate.

Live ladder (coded as mirefen — per-rung unlocks & cost):

Hoarfrost Hold  Farm

Environment. Deep snow and glacier ice thawed into warm ground. Sheltered-hollow rings cling to hot-spring warmth.

Crux · special. Warmth is the frontier, not space: bonfires extend the thaw zone. Board resources hide & amber.

Hazard · boss. Hard freeze, blizzard whiteout, ice spike, avalanche · [[bosses:frostmaw]] — freezes from outside in; thaw outward with bonfires and harvest 30 [[resources:plank|oak logs]] before the spring ices over.

Export. Warmth-radius / preserves bonus. Landmark: cairn & firepit → spring shrine → aurora bell → the Warm Hall.

Ladder (proposed, 4 tiers): Frostward Camp (3) → Snowhollow Hamlet (6) → Hoarfrost Village (10) → Auroral Hold (15); scales on [[resources:plank|plank]] + cured_meat / hide / amber + coins.

Sunspire Oasis  Farm

Environment. Encroaching dunes pushed back and irrigated into green oasis rings. Oasis-rings + caravan spokes.

Crux · special. Mirage tiles disguise resources; greed-vs-certainty — bank stockpile into caravans for coin multipliers, or play safe. New resource spice (milled); board resource dates.

Hazard · boss. Sandstorm, drought, scorpion swarm, quicksand, mirage · The Sandwyrm (proposed) — tunnels beneath the board and surfaces to bury a moving 2×3 block; mine the target before it's buried.

Export. A caravan coin-multiplier. Landmark: wellhead → palm cistern → sundial spire → grand sundial → the Sunspire.

Ladder (proposed, 5 tiers): Wellspring Camp (3) → Caravan Halt (6) → Oasis Market (10) → Sunspire Town (14) → Spice Capital (20); scales on block + dates + spice + [[resources:gold_bar|gold]].

Aetherreach  Mine

Environment. Floating sky-isles tethered by bridges. Sky-archipelago — each rung tethers a new drifting islet.

Crux · special. Everything floats: untethered tiles drift one cell with the prevailing wind each turn (the wind rotates per season). Anchoring & chain-direction always matter. Board resource aether (a premium metal).

Hazard · boss. Gale, lightning, island-drift, cloud-leech · The Sky Leviathan (proposed) — circles the isles; each pass tilts the board and slides an edge toward the void. Deliver the cargo before three passes.

Export. Aether (premium metal). Landmark: mooring mast → sky-beacon → wind shrine → aether spire → the Sky Citadel.

Ladder (proposed, 5 tiers): Anchor Platform (3) → Skydock Hamlet (6) → Aether Village (10) → Cloudreach Town (14) → Sky Citadel (18); scales on [[resources:plank|plank]] + aether + [[resources:gold_bar|gold]].

Gravemoor  special

Environment. Blight-fog and bramble over ruins, cleansed into hallowed cobble. Reclaimed-grid — the only zone that grows by uncovering a buried grid, not pushing outward.

Crux · special. Reclamation, not conquest: a chapel projects a consecration radius; night is more dangerous than day — race the dark. New resources warding_salt & blessed_oil.

Hazard · boss. Blight spread, restless wisp, bramble, miasma · The Barrow-Lich (proposed) — haunts a fresh tile each turn as a curse-meter climbs; banish enough hauntings by chaining to break it.

Export. Consecration / curse-ward. Landmark: warding fire → broken shrine → consecrated bell → the Dawnlight.

Ladder (proposed, 4 tiers): Wardens' Camp (3) → Hallowed Hamlet (6) → Reclaimed Village (10) → Dawnhold (15); scales on block + warding_salt + blessed_oil + coins.
