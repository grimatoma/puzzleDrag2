The Settlement Atlas — every town in depth. The structural role (board, crux, win task, signature export) comes from the town arc; the environment, growth topology, hazard and boss come from the zone atlas. Seven required-spine towns, then five optional frontier zones.

PROPOSED  Spine towns keep plain names (Hearthwood, Two Rivers, Greenmeadow); the dramatic sites keep evocative names (Cinderhold, Gullcliff, Glimmerdeep, Goldgrass). Where a town exists in code its live Zones page is linked; bosses are already coded and linked too.

Signature buildings (export after a town is complete)

Each town's win raises a signature building whose effect then helps every town: the Caravan Yard founds others; the Quartermaster's Hall packs rations better; the Pit Canteen adds expedition turns; the Bait Shack boosts fishing; the Cattery manages pests; the Supply Depot drips idle goods anywhere. These six are proposed; the everyday roster they sit among ([[buildings:mill]], [[buildings:bakery]], [[buildings:forge]], [[buildings:granary]], [[buildings:workshop]], [[buildings:chapel]]…) is live — see the Buildings catalog and Systems.
