Open numbers

The balance levers still unset — detailed on Economy: coin pacing (arrives a touch fast), worker→chain magnitudes, the crafting-substance gate, late-arc ladder costs (towns 4–7 + frontier are provisional), and value-spread compression.

Decision log

  • Reconciled roster. One settlement set blending the town arc's structure with the zone atlas's environments: 7 required-spine towns + 5 optional frontier zones (Thornwild folded, its [[bosses:mossback]] lent to Greenmeadow).
  • Non-linear founding + soft, acyclic feed gates — dissolves the old softlock by construction; the no-softlock invariant is canon.
  • Finale = complete the required spine → build the Monument, retiring the "3 Hearth-Tokens" gate and the festival-larder terminal beat (kept as optional flavour).
  • Home ladder stays 4 rungs; proposed to rename the top rung City → Town and re-cost it to the zones 1–2 baseline.
  • One sell price per good (see [[systems:trade]]).
