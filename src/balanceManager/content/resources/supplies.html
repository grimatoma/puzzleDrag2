Supplies are expedition rations — compressed grain provisions packed at the [[buildings:kitchen]]. Each unit buys a turn of expedition time when you stock a mine or harbor run; the [[buildings:larder]], once built, stretches each ration further by adding extra expedition time per unit on top. The [[recipes:rec_supplies]] recipe converts a batch of [[resources:flour]] into one supply at the Kitchen, so the farm grain chain feeds directly into your expedition budget.

Supplies are the bridge resource that makes the farm worth running even after you unlock the mine: chains on the farm board fill the flour stockpile, the Kitchen converts flour into supplies, and supplies buy the turns you need to work stone, iron, and gem seams in the [[zones:quarry]]. Without a supply stockpile, mine entry is possible but turn-limited.

Supplies are the baseline expedition food — accessible from the first Kitchen unlock. Higher-tier rations like [[resources:cured_meat]] and [[resources:iron_ration]] grant more turns per unit, making them worth pursuing once your supply lines are established.
