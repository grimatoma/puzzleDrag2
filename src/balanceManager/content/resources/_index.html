Resources are the goods that live in your inventory. They never appear on the board — instead, completing a tile chain credits a resource of the family's type (wheat chains credit [[resources:flour]], stone chains credit [[resources:block]], and so on). Resources are the currency of the crafting economy: you spend them at buildings like the [[buildings:bakery]], [[buildings:kitchen]], and [[buildings:forge]] to craft refined goods worth more coins or needed as expedition provisions.

Each resource has a sell value at the market and may appear as an ingredient in one or more [[recipes:rec_bread|recipes]]. Farm resources such as [[resources:flour]], [[resources:eggs]], and [[resources:bread]] power the early-game loop; mine resources like [[resources:iron_bar]] and [[resources:block]] open the mid-game crafting tier; harbor resources fill out the trade ledger and unlock the [[buildings:smokehouse]] recipes.
