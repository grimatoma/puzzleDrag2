Iron Bar is the workhorse metal of the mid-game economy, smelted from [[tiles:tile_mine_iron_ore]] chains in the mine. Iron ore is the second most common ore type in the mine pool after stone, making iron bars a reliable accumulator for any player who runs mine sessions consistently.

Iron bars show up as inputs across the [[buildings:workshop]] recipe list — the Sickle, Rifle, Iron Pick, Auger, and Blast Charge all need iron — and they are indispensable at the [[buildings:forge]], where they combine with [[resources:coke]] and [[resources:gold_bar]] to produce high-value crafted goods like [[resources:iron_hinge]], [[resources:lantern]], and [[resources:goldring]]. The Forge itself costs iron bars to construct, so you cannot open the top crafting tier without first mining a meaningful iron seam.

Iron bars are also a building material: the [[buildings:observatory]] and several advanced structures require them alongside [[resources:block]], creating a natural gate that keeps high-tier civic buildings in the later settlement tiers where they belong.
