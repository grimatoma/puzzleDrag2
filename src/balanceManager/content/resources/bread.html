Bread is the staple food of the Vale and the first refined good worth building a supply chain for. You bake it from [[resources:flour]] and [[resources:eggs]] at the [[buildings:bakery]] via the [[recipes:rec_bread]] recipe — and flour itself comes from milling wheat tiles, so a loaf is the payoff of a whole farm pipeline: chain wheat → flour → bread.

Beyond selling for coins, bread matters because it is expedition food: each loaf buys turns on a mine or harbor run. That is the bridge that makes the farm worth running before you ever found a mine — grain feeds the expeditions that bring back [[resources:block]] and [[resources:iron_bar]]. Build the [[buildings:mill]] first to cut the flour a loaf needs by one.

See the [[buildings:bakery]] for what else it bakes, and [[npcs:mira]], the baker who asks for it in her orders.
