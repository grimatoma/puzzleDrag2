Flour is the first refined resource the farm yields, milled straight from grain chains. Chaining [[tiles:tile_grain_wheat]] (and its cousins — corn, buckwheat, rice, manna) credits flour into your inventory. It is the foundation of the baked-goods economy: every recipe at the [[buildings:bakery]] and [[buildings:kitchen]] starts with flour, and the [[recipes:rec_supplies]] recipe that packs expedition rations demands a sizable flour reserve.

The [[buildings:mill]] earns its keep here — once built, it shaves one flour off the [[recipes:rec_bread]] recipe, which compounds across a full run when you are baking at volume. Without the mill the bread pipeline is hungrier for grain and it takes longer to provision an expedition. Build the mill early and keep the grain chains rolling.

Flour feeds into [[resources:bread]] (via the Bakery), [[resources:supplies]] (via the Kitchen), and the higher-tier [[resources:festival_loaf]] and [[resources:iron_ration]] recipes that become relevant once you have a [[zones:quarry]] to provision.
