Block is the primary mine resource — the dressed stone you haul out of the [[zones:quarry]] and every deeper mine site. Chaining [[tiles:tile_mine_stone]] tiles credits blocks into your inventory; stone is the most common tile in the mine pool, so block accumulates quickly once you are running mine sessions regularly.

Block is a load-bearing material in the crafting economy: it appears in the cost of the [[buildings:bakery]], [[buildings:workshop]], [[buildings:forge]], and several mid-tier buildings, and it is a recipe input for tools crafted at the [[buildings:workshop]] — the Stone Hammer, the Hound, and the Scythe (full) all ask for block. The [[buildings:forge]] needs block alongside [[resources:iron_bar]] for its own construction, so stockpiling stone is one of the first priorities after you found a mine settlement.

Block is distinct from the special dirt tiles in the mine — those credit [[resources:dirt]], which feeds fertilizer and explosives recipes rather than construction costs.
