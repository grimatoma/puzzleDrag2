The Mine unlocks at level 2. Its tiles are stone, ore, and gems; the board plays the same drag-to-chain mechanic but with mine-specific hazards and a unique ore event.

Mysterious Ore

Surfacing Mysterious Ore opens a 5-turn countdown: chain it together with at least two dirt tiles before the timer expires to be rewarded a Rune. If the countdown expires, the ore degrades back to plain dirt.

Hazards

  • Cave-In buries a whole row in rubble — chain 3 or more stone tiles adjacent to the rubble row to clear it.
  • Gas Vent drifts a noxious cloud you disperse by chaining through it, and the Giant Mole tunnels along a 3-turn cycle, eating adjacent tiles.
  • Lava Flow has no direct counter — it spreads each turn, so mine resources clear of its path before it reaches them.

Hazards spawn at a small chance per board fill, never while a boss is active, and never two at once.
