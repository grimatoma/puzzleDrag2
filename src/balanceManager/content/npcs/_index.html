NPCs are the named characters who live and work in the Vale. Each one has a role and a voice — [[npcs:mira]] runs the [[buildings:bakery]], [[npcs:bram]] tends the [[buildings:forge]], [[npcs:tomas]] keeps the hives, [[npcs:liss]] stocks the apothecary, and [[npcs:wren]] scouts the roads. They show up in the orders system, asking for specific goods in exchange for coins; filling their requests builds the relationship and advances story beats.

NPCs are not summoned or hired — they are part of the Vale's permanent cast. Their dialogue lines are procedurally selected based on what they want and when, so the same face may ask for different goods across sessions.
