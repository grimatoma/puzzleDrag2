Buildings are permanent structures you construct in a settlement. Each building costs coins and raw resources to build, and many grant an ongoing ability — the [[buildings:mill]] reduces a bread recipe's flour cost, the [[buildings:granary]] raises your inventory cap and adds a turn, the [[buildings:bakery]] unlocks the full baked-goods menu, and the [[buildings:forge]] opens the high-value metal-smithing tier. Buildings unlock at settlement levels and some require that another building be present first.

Once built, a building is available for the lifetime of that settlement. Crafting stations (Bakery, Kitchen, Workshop, Forge, Larder, Smokehouse) appear here in the catalog and also drive the [[recipes:rec_bread|recipes]] system — you must have the station built before you can craft its recipes (instantly, when you have the inputs). Cosmetic and civic buildings (Inn, Clock Tower, Chapel, Observatory) count toward town-planning achievements and add flavour without occupying a recipe slot.
