The Mill is the first building worth rushing in any farm settlement. Its sole ability is deceptively powerful: it reduces the flour input of the [[recipes:rec_bread]] recipe by one, which means every loaf you bake from that point on is cheaper to produce. Over a long run the flour you save compounds into a meaningful turn advantage — grain chains you would have spent refilling the bakery go toward stockpiling [[resources:supplies]] or selling on the market instead.

Building the Mill requires planks alongside coins, so you need a handful of [[tiles:tile_tree_oak]] chains before you can raise it. Once built it pairs naturally with the [[buildings:bakery]]: the two together are the backbone of the farm provisioning loop, turning grain and eggs into bread loaves that feed your expedition budget for the [[zones:quarry]] and beyond.

The Mill has no recipes of its own — it is a passive modifier, always on once built. Think of it as a permanent efficiency upgrade for the [[npcs:mira|baker's]] supply chain.
