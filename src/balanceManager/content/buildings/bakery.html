The Bakery is the heart of the farm's refining chain. It turns [[resources:flour]] (plus a few [[resources:eggs]]) into baked goods you can sell for coins or pack as expedition food. Its keystone recipe is [[recipes:rec_bread]] — but it also bakes richer goods such as honey rolls and harvest pies as your kingdom widens.

The Bakery is run by [[npcs:mira]], whose orders frequently ask for the very goods baked here. Pair it with the [[buildings:mill]], which reduces the flour each loaf needs — the two together are the engine that makes a farm self-sustaining and provisions your first expedition into the [[zones:quarry]].
