The Forge is the apex crafting station of the metal economy. It is an expensive build — requiring coins, a substantial [[resources:block]] reserve, and a stack of [[resources:iron_bar]] — which naturally places it in the mid-to-late settlement tier after you have run the [[zones:quarry]] long enough to stockpile ore. Once raised, it unlocks the highest-value recipes in the catalog: cobble paths, iron hinges, lanterns, gold rings, gem crowns, iron frames, and stonework.

All Forge recipes draw on mine resources. [[resources:coke]] (from coal chains) fuels the smelting process across multiple recipes; [[resources:gold_bar]] and [[resources:cut_gem]] feed the jewellery tier. [[npcs:bram]], the smith, is the NPC tied to the Forge — his orders often call for the very goods it produces.

Note that "forge" also appears on the map as the zone Black Forge, a separate mine-type settlement. The building and the zone share a name but are distinct entities; the wiki disambiguates them by their concept prefix.
