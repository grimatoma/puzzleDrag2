The Kitchen is the expedition-prep station of the farm settlement. Its primary recipe converts a batch of [[resources:flour]] into [[resources:supplies]] — the rations that fund mine and harbor sessions. Without the Kitchen there is no clean way to turn a surplus grain harvest into expedition turns, making it an essential building for anyone who wants to work the [[zones:quarry]] or other mine sites at scale.

The Kitchen also hosts the [[resources:iron_ration]] recipe (a high-density field ration for longer expeditions) once the required inputs become available. It is built after the early civic tier — its cost runs to bread, dirt, and iron (shown above) — and unlocks at settlement level 2, so it typically comes online around the time you are first eyeing the mine. [[npcs:wren]], the scout, is the NPC most associated with the road provisions the Kitchen produces.

Pair the Kitchen with the [[buildings:mill]] and [[buildings:bakery]] to close the full farm provisioning loop: grain chains → flour → supplies and bread → expedition turns. The [[buildings:larder]] further boosts the turn value of each supply unit once built.
