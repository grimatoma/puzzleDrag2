Systems are how the game actually works — the rules underneath the catalogs. Everything begins on the board: you [[systems:chaining|chain]] matching tiles to merge them and earn goods, and long chains [[systems:promotion|promote]] tiles up their family ladder. What the board hands you next is governed by the [[systems:spawn-pool|spawn pool]], which bends with the biome, the season, and the buildings you've raised.

From there the goods flow outward — [[systems:crafting|crafting]] refines them at station buildings, [[systems:inventory|storage]] caps how much you can stockpile, and [[systems:trade|trade]] turns the surplus into coins and townsfolk bonds. The larger subsystems each keep their own section below — Seasons, Workers, Tools, Buildings, Zones and Bosses — whose landing pages explain the mechanic and then list every entity in it.
