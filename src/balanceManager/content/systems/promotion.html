Tile promotion is what turns a good chain into a growing board. When a chain reaches a family's length threshold, the merge doesn't only pay out — it leaves behind an upgraded tile, the next rung up that family's ladder. Keep chaining and tiles climb from their humblest form toward their most valuable one.

Promotion is welded to [[systems:chaining]]: the longer the chain, the further up the ladder it can push, so the same skill that maximises yield also drives upgrades. Higher rungs are worth more both when chained again and when sold through [[systems:trade]].

The thresholds aren't fixed in stone. Workers such as the [[workers:farmer|Farmer]] trim a tile off their category's chain — the Farmer shortens grain, the [[workers:miner|Miner]] stone — so those families promote with shorter runs; promotion specialists go further still, making a long chain spill a bonus tile of the next family up.
