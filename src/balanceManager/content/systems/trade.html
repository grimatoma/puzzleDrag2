Trade is how goods become coins. The market buys your surplus resources at each good's sell value, turning a full [[systems:inventory|inventory]] into spendable coin for founding settlements, buying tools, and climbing tier ladders. Refined goods from [[systems:crafting|crafting]] fetch far more than the raw tiles they came from.

Alongside the open market, townsfolk place orders — a request for a specific good in exchange for coins and goodwill. Filling an order for [[npcs:mira|Mira]] the baker or [[npcs:bram|Bram]] the smith (and others like [[npcs:wren|Wren]], [[npcs:liss|Sister Liss]] and [[npcs:tomas|Old Tomas]]) raises your bond with them, and a higher bond multiplies the rewards their future orders pay out.

Trade is the sink that closes the loop the board opens: [[systems:chaining|chain]] goods, [[systems:crafting|refine]] them, then sell or deliver. Coin-focused workers and rising bonds compound what each delivery is worth.
