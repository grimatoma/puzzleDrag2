Tools are one-shot board interventions. You spend a tool to fire its power — clearing tiles, transforming them, reshuffling the board, or biasing what spawns next — and then it's gone until you obtain another. They're the pressure-release valve for a board that has stalled, and the way to set up a big [[systems:chaining|chain]] on demand.

The catalog spans gentle helpers to heavy artillery: the [[tools:axe|Axe]] fells every tree tile at once (its [[toolPowers:clear_category|clear-category]] power), the [[tools:bomb|Bomb]] detonates a 3×3 area ([[toolPowers:area_blast|area-blast]]), the [[tools:shuffle|Reshuffle Horn]] redeals the whole board, and the [[tools:fertilizer|Fertilizer]] leans the [[systems:spawn-pool|spawn pool]] toward grain for a turn. Some tools even answer the board's threats directly — the [[tools:cat|Cat]] clears [[hazards:rats|rat]] hazards in one move.
