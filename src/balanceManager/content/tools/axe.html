The Axe is the quickest way to cash in a board full of trees. One use sweeps every tree tile on the current board — oaks, birches, willows, firs, cypresses, and palms — dropping each log tile directly into your inventory in a single action without spending a turn. It is the tool of choice when the board has filled with tree tiles and you want to clear them in one go without chaining species one at a time.

The Axe is crafted at the [[buildings:workshop]] for a single [[resources:block]], making it one of the most accessible tools in the catalog — just a handful of stone chains and a Workshop build away. [[npcs:wren]] sometimes calls for planks via orders, and the Axe is the fastest way to answer that request when the farm board is tree-heavy.

The Axe complements the [[buildings:sawmill]] (which grants an extra plank per oak chain) — use the Sawmill for sustained oak farming and the Axe for a burst clear when you need planks fast or want to reset the board to a different tile family.
