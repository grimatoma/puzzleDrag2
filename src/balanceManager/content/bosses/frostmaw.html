Frostmaw is the winter keeper-boss — a frozen titan that stirs in the deep winter wood, its icy breath threatening every hearth in the Vale. To drive it back you must gather logs quickly, chaining oak tiles before the cold claims the village.

Its signature pressure is a board modifier: two columns freeze solid and cannot be chained until thawed, squeezing your usable board down while the clock runs. Defeating it within its season window pays out coins scaled to your margin plus a rune.

Frostmaw is one of six seasonal bosses (alongside [[bosses:quagmire]], [[bosses:ember_drake]], [[bosses:old_stoneface]], [[bosses:mossback]], and [[bosses:storm]]). As noted on the Progression page, bosses are currently PARTIAL — fully defined with targets, modifiers, and rewards, but triggered manually or by story beats rather than by an automatic cooldown loop.
