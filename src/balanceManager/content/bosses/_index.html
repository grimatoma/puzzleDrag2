Bosses are seasonal challenge encounters. Each is tied to a season and sets a target (gather N of a specific resource) within a fixed turn window, while imposing a board modifier that changes how the board plays — freezing columns, blocking cells with rubble, hiding tiles face-down, or raising the minimum chain length. Beat the target before the window closes and you earn coins (scaled to how far past the target you push) plus a rune.

There are six, spread across the seasons — Frostmaw in winter, Quagmire and Mossback in spring, the Ember Drake and the Storm in summer, and Old Stoneface in autumn (each is listed below). In the fiction they overlap with the land-spirit keepers: a "Drive Out" choice resolves through the same boss-trial engine.

Status: bosses are PARTIAL today — fully defined with targets, modifiers, and rewards, and they run in trials and tests, but they are triggered manually (debug) or by story beats rather than by an automatic day-cooldown loop.
