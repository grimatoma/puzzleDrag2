Hazards are threats that spawn on the board mid-session and disrupt your chaining. The farm has three: Rats invade the granary and devour adjacent plant tiles each turn (chain rat tiles together to clear them); Wolves hunt your bird tiles and must be driven off with a Rifle or scattered temporarily with a Hound; and Fire (currently behind a feature flag) spreads to adjacent tiles and is extinguished by chaining any fire cells. Only one farm hazard is active at a time.

The mine has its own roster: Cave-Ins bury a whole row in rubble, Gas Vents spread a noxious cloud on a short countdown, Lava flows from a breach and has no direct counter — it spreads each turn, so you must clear resources out of its path before it reaches them, and Moles burrow through stone tiles. Mine hazards also follow a single-active cap. Tools from the [[buildings:workshop]] — bombs, drills, water pumps — are the fastest counter to the more severe mine threats.
