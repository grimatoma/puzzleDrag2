A cave-in is the mine's row-denial hazard: a tunnel collapse buries an entire row of the board in rubble, and every tile in that row becomes impassable until you dig it out. It cannot be chained through, so it effectively shrinks your usable board and chokes your mining run.

How it works

Cave-ins appear only on the [[boardKinds:mine]] board. After each board refill there is a 5% chance for some mine hazard to spawn, and the cave-in carries a spawn weight of 25 in that roll. Hazards never spawn while a boss is active, and only one hazard is ever on the board at a time. Once placed, a cave-in has no timer — it sits on its row until you clear it.

Clearing it

Chain 3 or more [[tiles:tile_mine_stone]] tiles in a row directly adjacent to the buried row. A single qualifying stone chain shores up the tunnel and clears all of the rubble at once, so position your chain in the neighbouring row rather than trying to dig the rubble itself.
