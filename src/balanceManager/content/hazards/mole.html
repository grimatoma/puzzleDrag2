The Giant Mole is a roaming mine hazard — a burrowing beast that chews through your resource tiles as it tunnels across the board. Rather than blocking the board, it actively eats into your hard-won ore one tile at a time.

How it works

The mole appears only on the [[boardKinds:mine]] board and is the rarest mine hazard, with a spawn weight of just 15 in the shared 5%-per-refill roll. It moves on a 3-turn cycle: on each of those turns it consumes one adjacent (non-rubble) resource tile, and when the cycle resets it hops to a new free adjacent cell and starts digging again.

Clearing it

There is no tool or chain that removes the mole directly — you have to limit the damage. Clear the tiles around its current position so it has nothing valuable to eat, and chain away the ore in its likely path before it burrows into it.
