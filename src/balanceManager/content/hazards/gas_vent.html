Gas Vent is the most common mine hazard — a crack opens and a noxious cloud drifts over a patch of the board. Unlike rubble or lava, gas tiles stay fully chainable; the cloud is a timed threat rather than a wall.

How it works

Gas vents spawn only on the [[boardKinds:mine]] board, sharing the 5%-per-refill hazard roll with the other mine dangers, where it carries the heaviest spawn weight of 40. The vent comes with a 3-turn timer. If you let that timer run out, the gas costs you a wasted turn as you cough through it, and only then disperses.

Clearing it

Chain through any tiles inside the cloud before the timer expires — that disperses the gas immediately and saves you the wasted turn. Because the affected tiles remain matchable, the counter is simply to route a normal chain through the cloud.
