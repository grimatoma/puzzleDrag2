Wolves are the farm's predator hazard. A pack slips onto the board and hunts your bird tiles, eating into your egg and turkey supply one tile per wolf each turn until you drive them off.

How it works

Wolves appear only on the [[boardKinds:farm]] board, and only when your farm is bird-rich — more than 30 eggs banked, or more than 5 [[tiles:tile_bird_turkey]] banked. When that condition holds there is a 6% chance per board refill for a wolf to spawn, up to 2 wolves active at once (and never alongside another farm hazard or a boss). Each turn, every un-scared wolf devours one adjacent bird tile.

Clearing it

Two workshop tools answer the pack. A [[tools:rifle]] drives off every wolf permanently, ending the hazard outright. A [[tools:hound]] is the cheaper, temporary option — it scares the wolves for 5 turns, during which they stop eating, buying you time to chain their target birds out of reach.
