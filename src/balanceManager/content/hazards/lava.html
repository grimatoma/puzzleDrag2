Lava Flow is the mine's creeping-destruction hazard. A seam cracks open as a single molten cell, then spreads across the board, converting and destroying whatever resource it touches. Lava tiles are impassable and cannot be chained.

How it works

Lava spawns only on the [[boardKinds:mine]] board through the shared 5%-per-refill hazard roll, with a spawn weight of 20. It begins as one cell and, every turn, spreads into a random orthogonally-adjacent empty tile — overwriting that tile with lava and obliterating any resource that was there. There is no timer and no countdown: it keeps advancing until it is boxed in by the board edges or other lava.

Clearing it

There is no direct counter — you cannot extinguish lava by chaining it. The only play is to mine the resources out of its path before the flow reaches them, racing the spread to salvage as much ore as you can.
