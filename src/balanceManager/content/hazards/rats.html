Rats are the farm's infestation hazard. They overrun the granary and gnaw at your crops, eating the plant tiles next to them and spreading their damage the longer they are allowed to linger.

How it works

Rats appear on the [[boardKinds:farm]] board and fall under the farm's single-active-hazard cap, so they never share the board with [[hazards:fire]], [[hazards:wolf]], or an active boss. Every turn, each rat eats an adjacent plant tile — so an unaddressed nest steadily chews through your harvest.

Clearing it

Chain 3 or more rat tiles together to clear them out. Exterminating the nest also pays a bounty of 5 coins per rat removed, rewarding you for dealing with a larger swarm in a single chain.
