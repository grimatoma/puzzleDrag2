Fire is the farm's spreading hazard. A blaze breaks out in the fields and, left unchecked, jumps from tile to tile, burning away your crops. Unlike most threats it actually rewards a quick response — extinguishing it pays out a little coin.

How it works

Fire spawns only on the [[boardKinds:farm]] board, at a 4% chance per board refill. The farm enforces a single-active-hazard cap, so fire never co-spawns with [[hazards:wolf]] or [[hazards:rats]], nor while a boss is active. Each existing fire cell then has a 50% chance every turn to spread into an adjacent empty tile, destroying the resource there, and the blaze is capped at 3 cells total.

Clearing it

Chain any fire tiles to extinguish them. Putting out the fire also hands you a small coin bonus — 2 coins per fire tile cleared — so dousing a larger blaze in one chain is both safer and more profitable.
