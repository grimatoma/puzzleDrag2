Saltspray Harbor is the sea hold — a weather-bleached pier where nets come up full of sardines, mackerel, and clams. It opens the third and final board type: the fishing expedition. Like a mine it is supply-funded rather than season-structured, but it runs on a tide cycle a mine never has — which fish surface, and how often, shifts as the tide turns, giving the harbor a rhythm all its own. PARTIAL The board runs; it is held back as a progression slot so the sea can grow into its own identity later.

Chaining the catch credits sea resources: fish go to [[resources:fish_fillet]], clams to [[resources:sea_shells]], kelp to [[resources:fish_oil]], and oysters to the harbor's signature [[resources:pearls]] — used in high-value recipes and required, as a Tidesinger's Pearl, to unlock the Old Capital. Its buildings are coast-specific: the [[buildings:harbor_dock]], [[buildings:fishmonger]], [[buildings:smokehouse]], and [[buildings:lighthouse]].

The harbor opens as part of the post-Town-2 branch: it carries a requiresZoneTier on the quarry, so the coast/fishing line becomes available once you have established the mine. Unusually, the harbor is reached straight from [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]] on the map — discovered early but tier-gated, so it waits until you are ready. Completing it earns the Tidesinger's Pearl token toward the Old Capital. For where it sits in the journey, see the Direction page.
