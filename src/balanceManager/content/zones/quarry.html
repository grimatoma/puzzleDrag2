Cracked Quarry is the first mine zone — a wide, shattered pit that opens the ore and stone economy. Its board runs the mine tile pool: stone is common, iron ore and copper ore appear at middling rates, coal and gems at lower rates. Chaining stone tiles credits [[resources:block]]; iron ore chains credit [[resources:iron_bar]]; coal goes to [[resources:coke]]. The Quarry is the primary source of building materials that the later settlement tiers — and the [[buildings:forge]] — demand.

Entry requires coins for each session, and you need expedition rations ([[resources:supplies]] or [[resources:bread]]) to fund a meaningful turn budget. Pack the Kitchen before you set out: the [[buildings:kitchen]] at [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]] converts flour into supplies, and supplies buy the turns you need to work a deep seam. The Quarry's hazards — cave-ins, gas vents, and moles — add urgency; the [[buildings:workshop]] tools (Stone Hammer, Auger, Blast Charge, Explosives) are the fastest answers.

The Quarry's available buildings include the [[buildings:kitchen]], [[buildings:workshop]], [[buildings:forge]], [[buildings:observatory]], and the [[buildings:barn]] (which preserves the mine board between sessions). Completing it earns the Pact Iron token toward the Old Capital arc.
