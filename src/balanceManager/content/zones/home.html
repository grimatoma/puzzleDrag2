Hearthwood Vale is your home hearth — the first settlement, auto-founded at the start, and the game's tutorial. It is a farm zone: you arrive to a cold hearth kept alive by [[npcs:wren]], and your first chains relight it. Everything you learn here — dragging chains, hitting upgrade thresholds, filling orders, refining grain into [[resources:flour]] and then [[resources:bread]] — is the foundation for every ruin you rebuild afterward.

The Vale teaches the farm loop in isolation: there is no mine here. The plan is for it to climb the tier ladder, with productive buildings like the [[buildings:mill]] and [[buildings:bakery]] doubling as the spawners that widen the board. When the Vale is restored, you raise the wagon that wakes the next ruin — the first of which is the [[zones:quarry]].

For how the Vale fits the larger rebuild arc, see the Direction page; for the fiction of the banked hearth, see Story.
