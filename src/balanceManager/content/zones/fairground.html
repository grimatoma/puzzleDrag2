Drifter's Fairground is a rolling fair of music, trinkets, and seasonal contests — a board-less festival node rather than a settlement to rebuild. There is no puzzle board; instead it offers rotating rewards and the occasional story fragment, a lighter stop between the harder zones. STUB The recurring festival offers are still coming soon.

It opens at level 3 — the same gate as [[zones:harbor|Saltspray Harbor]] — reached onward from the mines at [[zones:quarry|Cracked Quarry]] or [[zones:caves|Lanternlit Caves]], and it bridges toward the deep zones, the [[zones:forge|Black Forge]] and [[zones:pit|The Pit]]. For where it sits in the overall flow, see the Direction page.
