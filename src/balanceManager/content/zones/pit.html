The Pit is a boss arena, not a settlement — something stirs in the dark, and you come here to fight it rather than to farm or mine. There is no puzzle board to rebuild; instead the Pit stages the game's most challenging encounters and the rare loot they drop. Bring your strongest chains. PARTIAL Bosses such as [[bosses:frostmaw]] exist with their own targets, board modifiers, and rewards, but the Pit's full battle loop is still being built.

The deepest reachable node before the finale, the Pit opens at level 6, approached from [[zones:fairground|Drifter's Fairground]] or the [[zones:forge|Black Forge]], and it adjoins the [[zones:oldcapital|Old Capital]]. For where it sits in the overall progression, see the Direction page.
