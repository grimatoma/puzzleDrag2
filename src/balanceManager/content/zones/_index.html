Zones are the map locations you travel to and rebuild. Each zone is a settlement site: it has a board type (farm, mine, harbor, or none), a turn budget per session, an entry cost in coins, and a set of buildings you can construct there. Your home zone is [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]], which is pre-founded and teaches the farm loop. Beyond it, [[zones:meadow|Greenmeadow]] offers sun-drenched fields for early harvests, and [[zones:quarry|Cracked Quarry]] opens the mine economy once you are ready for ore and stone.

Founding a new settlement costs coins and unlocks that zone's full building list and board. Completing a settlement — enough buildings up, keeper resolved — earns a Hearth-Token that advances the Old Capital arc.
