Zones are the map locations you travel to and rebuild. Each zone is a settlement site with a board type (farm, mine, harbor, or none), a turn budget per session, an entry cost in coins, and a set of buildings you can construct there. Your home zone is [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]], pre-founded and the site of the tutorial. Completing a settlement — enough buildings up and keeper resolved — earns a Hearth-Token that advances the Old Capital arc.

The zone network is semi-linear and teaching-first: at level 1 only [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]] and [[zones:meadow|Greenmeadow]] are reachable, so the opening is a focused farm corridor. Level 2 opens [[zones:orchard|Wild Orchard]] and the path through [[zones:crossroads|The Crossroads]] to [[zones:quarry|Cracked Quarry]], the first mine. Level 3 adds [[zones:harbor|Saltspray Harbor]] and [[zones:fairground|Drifter's Fairground]]. Levels 4–6 lead through [[zones:caves|Lanternlit Caves]], [[zones:forge|Black Forge]], and [[zones:pit|The Pit]], until all three biomes are complete and [[zones:oldcapital|The Old Capital]] can be reached. For the full ordered journey and what each stop teaches, see the Direction page.
