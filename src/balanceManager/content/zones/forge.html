The Black Forge is the mastery mine — a roaring smithy at the foot of the mountain, where heroes' tools are born. It has the steepest entry cost of any zone and, in return, the richest mine output: this is where boss-tier resources are smelted and the most advanced crafted goods are made. The board runs the mine pool like the [[zones:quarry|Quarry]] and [[zones:caves|Caves]], but everything here is pitched for an experienced miner — and notably, the Forge's furnaces burn so hot that the usual cave hazards keep their distance.

Reached at level 5 by way of [[zones:fairground|Drifter's Fairground]], the Forge sits near the end of the road, with [[zones:pit|The Pit]] and ultimately the [[zones:oldcapital|Old Capital]] beyond it. Its building list centers on the [[buildings:forge]] and [[buildings:workshop]] for high-tier crafting. Completing the zone earns a Pact-Iron Hearth-Token toward the Old Capital.
