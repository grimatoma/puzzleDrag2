Greenmeadow is the first farm settlement beyond the Vale — a sun-drenched expanse of fields that is friendlier to new players than the more complex zones further along the road. Its board is a standard farm board with the same grass, grain, tree, and bird families as [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]], and the same upgrade map that lets grain chains climb toward vegetables and fruits. No mine, no harbor, no hazards by default — just an open field ready to be worked.

Greenmeadow is reached from the map by traveling north from Hearthwood Vale. Its entry cost keeps it accessible early, and its building list — [[buildings:mill]], [[buildings:granary]], [[buildings:bakery]], [[buildings:larder]], [[buildings:stable]], [[buildings:apiary]], and others — covers most of the core farm economy. It is a good place to found a second grain-and-bread operation before you are ready to tackle the [[zones:quarry]].

As a farm-type zone, completing Greenmeadow — buildings up and keeper resolved — earns the Heirloom Seed token, one of the three needed to unlock the Old Capital.
