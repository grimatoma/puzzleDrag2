Wild Orchard is the richer of the early farm zones — tangled rows of fruit trees that reward patience. Its board runs the same farm families as [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]] and [[zones:meadow|Greenmeadow]], but the tile mix skews heavily toward fruits and trees rather than grain, and its upgrade map lets chains climb into the higher crop tiers. This is the place to chain long: fruit feeds [[resources:pie]] and the orchard's flowers feed [[resources:honey]] — the kind of high-value goods the temperate farms only trickle out.

The Orchard opens at level 2, reached south from [[zones:home|Hearthwood Vale]] — one of the two branches that crack the map open once the teaching corridor is behind you (the other leads through [[zones:crossroads|The Crossroads]] to the first mine). It is an optional deepening of your farming rather than a required step, and a good place to found a second farm before committing to ore. Its buildings — [[buildings:mill]], [[buildings:granary]], [[buildings:bakery]], [[buildings:apiary]], [[buildings:sawmill]], and more — cover the full farm economy.

As a farm-type zone, completing Wild Orchard — buildings up and keeper resolved — earns the Heirloom Seed token, one of the three Hearth-Tokens needed to unlock the Old Capital. For where it sits in the overall journey, see the Direction page.
