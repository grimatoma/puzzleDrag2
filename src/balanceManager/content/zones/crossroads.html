The Crossroads is a windswept junction rather than a settlement — a board-less waypoint where strangers, rumors, and the occasional encounter meet. There is no puzzle board here and nothing to harvest; what the Crossroads offers is passage and story. It is the hinge that connects the farm cluster to the mine cluster.

You reach it at level 2 from either [[zones:meadow|Greenmeadow]] or [[zones:orchard|Wild Orchard]], and from it the road forks onward to the mines — [[zones:quarry|Cracked Quarry]] first, with [[zones:caves|Lanternlit Caves]] beyond. Think of it less as a destination than as the moment the game stops being farm-only and the wider map begins to open. For the full progression, see the Zone flow page.
