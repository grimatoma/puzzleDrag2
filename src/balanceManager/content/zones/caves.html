Lanternlit Caves is a deeper, more dangerous mine than the [[zones:quarry|Cracked Quarry]] — twisting tunnels lit by old miners' lanterns, where rare gems hide in the dark. Its board runs the mine tile pool with gems at the bottom of the rate table; chaining them credits [[resources:cut_gem]], and the richest seams reach [[resources:gold_bar]]. Like every mine it is an expedition: pack [[resources:supplies]] before you go, because turns are bought with food and the session ends when it runs out.

What sets the Caves apart is the lava hazard, which the Quarry never sees. Lava spreads cell by cell if you leave it alone, steadily eating into the board — so the central skill here is keeping your ore output up while you mine around the creep. The Quarry's other hazards (cave-ins, gas vents, moles) return as well. The [[buildings:workshop]] tools are your fastest answers, and the [[buildings:observatory]] sharpens your gem yields.

Reached at level 4 by way of [[zones:crossroads|The Crossroads]], the Caves are a mid-to-late stop on the road to the [[zones:forge|Black Forge]]. Completing the zone earns a Pact-Iron token toward the Old Capital. For where it sits in the journey, see the Direction page.
