The Old Capital is the first hearth of the old kingdom — dark for an age — and the game's meta-finale. It is not travelled to like an ordinary zone: it stays visible on the map from the very start but remains locked until you hold one Hearth-Token of each biome type — a Heirloom Seed from a completed farm zone, a Pact-Iron token from a mine, and a Tidesinger's Pearl from the [[zones:harbor|harbor]].

Holding all three opens the node, and you make the long return to renew the Hollow Pact at the place where it was first made — the centennial gathering the founders missed a hundred years ago. STUB The map node and the three-token unlock gate exist today; the travel flow and the finale itself are intentionally left open until the rest of the game's identity is firmer.
