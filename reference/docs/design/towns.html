Settlement Atlas

Every settlement in depth. The structural role (board, crux, win task, signature export) comes from the town arc; the environment, growth topology, hazard, boss and tier-ladder costs come from the Zone Atlas. Seven required-spine towns, then five optional frontier zones.

Canonical · reconciled roster · tier-ladder costs mirror zones/ data & Economy

📐
Tone & naming. Spine towns keep the arc's plain names (Hearthwood, Two Rivers, Greenmeadow); the dramatic sites keep the Atlas's evocative names (Cinderhold, Gullcliff, Glimmerdeep, Goldgrass). A uniform "grounded" skin (Rivermeet, Millbrook…) exists in zones/grounded.html if a cozier register is wanted — it is a global rename, not a redesign.

The required spine

Seven towns the finale needs, in the order the map suggests. Each adds at most one new board, one crux, one win task, and a signature building that exports a bonus to every other town once complete.

Town 1 · home Hearthwood Vale Farm

Environment
A gentle forgotten vale around a cold hearth. Radial growth — the settlement spreads outward from the evolving hearth-landmark as wilderness recedes.
Crux
The tutorial home. Teaches every verb — chain, refine, fill orders, raise a House and hire a worker, tier up — with no pressure. Your home forever.
Hazard · boss
None. A safe teacher.
Win task
Save the coins and build the Caravan at the Caravan Yard, then roll out to your first new site.
Signature export
Caravan Yard — the means to found every other town.

Town 2 Two Rivers FarmMine

Environment
A calm valley where two forks meet. Twin-fork growth — the town fills the land between the rivers, a mill on each bank.
Crux
The combination. The first mine, deliberately hazard-free: you learn to pack farm food into expedition turns and bring back ore & metal. The quiet "aha" of the whole economy.
Hazard · boss
None (calm teacher).
Win task
Fill a grand order that demands both farm and mine goods at once.
Signature export
Quartermaster's Hall — packs food into rations more efficiently, everywhere.
Founding
A resource caravan — 12×plank 10×bread 8×hay (farm goods, not coins).

Town 3 Cinderhold FarmMine

Environment
Glowing lava fields cooling into obsidian terraces. Switchback-terrace growth — hairpin roads climb the slope, each rung claiming a higher cooled shelf.
Crux
Mine-hazard management. A rising heat meter channels your routes; the frontier metal obsidian is only won at the dangerous edge.
Hazard · boss
Heat tiles, ash, ember-rain, tremors · Ember Drake — heat pools on the lowest terrace first; anything left two turns burns. Land 3 iron bars before it floods.
Win task
Best the capstone boss.
Signature export
Pit Canteen — +ration-turns on any mine expedition, everywhere.

Town 4 Gullcliff FarmMineFish

Environment
A bare sea-cliff carved into railed terraces and harbour shelves. Vertical-cliff growth — shelves stacked and linked by stairs and a cargo lift; the harbour floods and drains with the tide.
Crux
The full stack. The first three-board town: farm bait + mine tackle fund the fishery. Vertical logistics — where you mine (low) and craft (high) sit on different levels.
Hazard · boss
Squall, riptide, rogue wave, gull raids · The Storm — demands 4+ fish chains; the lighthouse sweep briefly calms one column per pass. Land 6 fillets before the window closes.
Win task
The Regatta.
Signature export
Bait Shack — a standing fishing bonus, everywhere.

A rich, broad meadow — the most generous farmland yet. Radial growth around a great barn.

Rat infestation · Mossback (lent from Thornwild) — hides tiles under moss; a roaming sun-shaft reveals them. Gather the harvest before the meadow swallows it.

Town 6 Glimmerdeep Mine only

Environment
A chain of unlit caverns opened one by one. Linked-chambers growth — each rung lights and clears the next chamber; cart-rails ferry resources between them. Light is life: unlit tiles can't be chained until a lantern reaches them.
Crux
Provision under scarcity. The deliberate exception: a mine with no farm. You must import food from your other towns to keep the expeditions running — a logistics test, not a farming one.
Hazard · boss
Cave-in, gas vent, sinkhole · Old Stoneface — drops rubble and snuffs the nearest lantern; keep the chamber lit while clearing 20 stone tiles.
Win task
Fill the great shipment under that scarcity.
Signature export
Supply Depot — an idle resource drip, everywhere. The most coveted cross-zone unlock.

Town 7 · finale Goldgrass · The Golden Capital FarmMineFish

Environment
Golden prairie urbanised into grand civic districts, ringed by permanent fields. Axial-boulevard growth — a monumental central avenue and ring road; districts fill the quadrants. The widest layout in the game.
Crux
Everything at once. All three boards at the grandest scale; the capstone of scale and prestige.
Hazard · boss
Locust, wildfire, drought, stampede · Goldhorn — a plains-bull that charges every few turns and tramples a full band; herd the right resource into the stockpens before it flattens them.
Win task
Build the Monument — the finale (see Progression).
Signature
The Monument itself — wakes the kingdom.

The optional frontier

Five further sites you may found on reach + coins. None is required for the finale; each ships its own hazard, boss, tier ladder, and a cross-zone export worth the detour.

frontier Mirefen Hollow Fish

Environment · topology
Black-water fen and reed beds planked into buildable hummocks · ribbon-stilt boardwalk (spreads island to island).
Hazard · boss
Tidewater flood, miasma fog, leech bloom, will-o'-wisp · Quagmire — a high/low tide cycle: harvest at high tide, stranded at low. Tide-reading is the skill.
Export
A tide-reading idle drip. New resource: fenmead (brewed, gates the top rung).

frontier Hoarfrost Hold Farm

Environment · topology
Deep snow and glacier ice thawed into warm ground · sheltered-hollow rings (cling to hot-spring warmth). Warmth is the frontier, not space: bonfires extend the thaw zone.
Hazard · boss
Hard freeze, blizzard whiteout, ice spike, avalanche · Frostmaw — freezes from outside in; thaw outward with bonfires and harvest 30 oak logs before the spring ices over.

Warmth-radius / preserves bonus. Board resources: hide, amber.

Environment · topology
Floating sky-isles tethered by bridges · sky-archipelago. Everything floats: untethered tiles drift one cell with the prevailing wind each turn (the wind rotates per season).
