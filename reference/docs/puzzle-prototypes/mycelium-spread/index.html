Mycelium Spread

Plant a spore and grow a living network. Your mycelium auto-harvests every resource it touches each tick — passive income — and you DRAG to direct its growth across the richest ground. The active chain becomes a seed for an idle, spreading organism.

Event log

How to play

  • Drag a path that starts on a glowing mycelium cell and crosses touching earth tiles (8 directions). Release to grow the network along it — costs 1 nutrient per new cell.
  • Out of nutrients mid-path? It grows as far as you can afford and tells you. Min path is one cell beyond the start.
  • Every tick (each drag, or the Grow a season button) every live cell auto-harvests one adjacent earth tile — its value banks as Nutrients + Score, and that tile becomes spent soil. The frontier may also spread for free, and starving cells die back.
  • Steer toward unharvested, high-value ground; keep your network fed; reinvest nutrients into more growth.

Why it is ours: It flips the loop: the active drag plants growth that then harvests ON ITS OWN over turns — an idle/territory layer grafted onto the chain. It echoes Puzzle Craft 2 "you choose where islands appear" placement and gives the game a passive-progress dimension steered by puzzle skill.
