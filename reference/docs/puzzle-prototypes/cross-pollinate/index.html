Event log

How to play

  • Drag across 3+ touching tiles of the same family (8 directions). Release to harvest — each plain tile scores 1.
  • The real play is routing: bend your chain around a clump of a different family. On release, every tile 8-adjacent to your chain (but not in it) is checked — if 2+ of them share one family, those tiles graft into premium ✦ hybrid blooms.
  • Hybrids are their own family. Drag 3+ ✦ blooms together for a triple-value payout. Each hybrid remembers the pair that made it (e.g. 🌾×🌸).
  • Tap New board to reseed. Chains shorter than 3 are ignored.

Why it is ours: It turns the board into a place where NEW tile types emerge from player routing — a discovery/combo layer — while reusing the exact adjacency + refill machinery the game already has for cross-collect. Same one drag verb; richer consequences.
