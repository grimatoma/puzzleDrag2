The headline finding · severity: blocker

The progression softlock

The circular dependency

Read it as a ring with no entry point. Each arrow is "needs" — follow them around and they close on themselves.

Three stacked locks

It is not one gate but three, layered — any single one would be awkward, together they are a hard wall. Each is read from the data and confirmed in the source.

The Bakery's own build cost needs a mine good

The Bakery — the only station that crafts bread — costs 8×plank 6×block 4×eggs. That block is a mine good a farm board never spawns, so you cannot even build the Bakery at home, let alone bake the bread the next rung wants.

src/constants.ts → BUILDINGS bakery {plank:8, block:6, eggs:4}

The Bakery only unlocks at Hamlet — whose gate is bread

Even with the goods, the Bakery is a Hamlet-tier unlock, and the Hamlet rung's tier-up cost itself demands 10×bread. So the building that makes bread is locked behind the rung that needs bread — a chicken-and-egg knot nested inside lock A.

src/features/cartography/data.ts → home tiers (Hamlet upgradeCost + unlocks)

The upper rungs demand mine & sea goods directly

Past Hamlet the home ladder asks for 20×block 14×coke 14×silver_bar (Village) and 30×iron_bar 13×gold_bar 1×jade (City). And every mine/fish zone that could supply them carries a requiresZoneTier gate routing back through the quarry — which itself needs home at City.

src/features/cartography/data.ts → home Village/City upgradeCost · quarry requiresZoneTier {home, tier 3}

Levers — for the designer, not applied here

Nothing below has been changed.

These are options to evaluate. Any one of them breaks the cycle; the cleanest fix is the smallest edit that opens a farm-only path to the first real rung.

1 · Re-cost the Bakery off block

Swap the Bakery's block for a farm good (e.g. hay_bundle) so bread becomes farm-craftable. The keystone edit — it unties locks A and B at once.

BUILDINGS bakery · constants.ts

2 · Unlock the Bakery at Outpost

Move the Bakery from a Hamlet unlock to an Outpost (tier 0) unlock, so the player can bake before the rung that needs bread.

home tiers[].unlocks · cartography/data.ts

3 · Re-gate the quarry earlier

Lower the quarry's requiresZoneTier from {home, tier 3} toward tier 0–1 so mine goods are reachable before the home ladder demands them.

quarry requiresZoneTier · cartography/data.ts:344

4 · Seed an early mine good

Grant or sell a small starter stock of block/iron_bar — open the latent market escape, or a quest/daily grant — so the first rungs have a first step.

market escape · see note above
