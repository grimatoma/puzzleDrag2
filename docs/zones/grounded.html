Grounded Settlements

Ten believable frontier towns for puzzleDrag2 — each a real place in the woods, plains, hills or coast, each defined by its own water feature, each growing from a handful of cabins into a town or city. Drawn on the real 40×30 game grid with the actual tileset (grass, dirt, water, forest, cottages, mill, docks) and lived-in townsfolk. Open any one and drag “Grow” to watch an outpost become a city.

puzzleDrag2 · grounded redo of the zone set · flat tileset mockups, pixel polish later · 2026-06-17

What each proves

Every settlement sits on a real reason to exist and grows the way a real town does — roads first, the ford then the bridge, the mill, the quays, the market square, the outer districts — with the forest/frontier receding as the visible progress bar. Together they exercise every water primitive the renderer supports: river bend, lake cove, creek + pond, sea coast + bay, two-river confluence, forest stream + millpond, mountain tarn + burn, braided fen channels, highland loch, and river-mouth estuary.
