How to play

  • Drag across 3+ touching tiles of the same crop (8 directions) and release to harvest. The 💧 river is terrain — you can't include it in a chain.
  • Every chain tile that touches the river (4-adjacent) lights up with a blue edge and adds a ×0.5 flow bonus. Hug the bank for huge scores.
  • Toggle Dig channel ⛏️, then tap a land tile next to existing water to convert it to river — spending one charge and widening your irrigation. You earn a new charge every few harvests.
  • After a harvest, crops cascade down each column and settle on top of the river cells (water never moves); fresh crops rain in from the top.

Why it is ours: It adds spatial terrain and a second, board-editing verb (irrigation) on top of chaining — you are no longer just reacting to a random board, you are shaping it. Reuses families, chains, cascade, and refill; the river is the only new primitive.
