How to play

  • Drag a path through touching tiles (8 directions). The path may cross any families — what matters is the order you pick them up. Minimum 2 tiles.
  • The Current path readout shows your live sequence (e.g. 🌾→💧→🌾) and lights up when it matches — or leads toward — a recipe.
  • Release to cook. An exact-order match is a Perfect brew worth ×2. The same ingredients in the wrong order still cook the dish at ×1. Anything else cooks nothing and keeps your tiles.
  • A successful brew consumes the path, the board cascades down, and fresh ingredients refill from the top.

Why it is ours: It promotes the game crafting recipes from a separate menu onto the board: you cook by routing through ingredients in sequence. "Match identical tiles" becomes "compose a recipe", directly extending the existing resource→recipe pipeline into the core puzzle.
