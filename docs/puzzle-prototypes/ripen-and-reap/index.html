Ripen & Reap

The same drag-a-chain-of-3+ verb — but every tile is alive on a clock. Crops grow 🌱→🌿→✨ripe→🥀rotten. Harvest at peak for the big yield; leave it too long and it rots and spreads to its neighbours. Now when you chain matters as much as what.

How to play

  • Drag across 3+ touching tiles of the same crop (8 directions). Release to harvest.
  • Yield scales with each tile's ripeness. A chain that is all ripe scores a ×1.5 peak bonus.
  • Every turn the field ages. Rotten 🥀 tiles spread to a random neighbour each turn — clear them (even for 0 points) or lose the patch.

Why it's ours: the existing game already drags chains and refills a board. This adds a single new dimension — a per-tile clock — that turns a flat match into a timing-and-triage puzzle without a second puzzle verb. It reuses cascade/refill, family chains, and the resource ladder exactly as-is.
