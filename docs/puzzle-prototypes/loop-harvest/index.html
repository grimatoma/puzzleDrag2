Event log

How to play

  • Drag across touching tiles of the same crop (8 directions) to lay a fence — a hedge of one family.
  • Once the fence is 6+ long and curls back next to where it began, the start tile glows green ⟲. Release on (or next to) it to close the loop.
  • The board floods in from the edges; whatever the fence seals off is the interior. You reap the interior and the fence. Score jumps with inside² — a fat 6-tile field beats a thin 12-tile snake.
  • A 4-sealed loop holds water; a fence that only touches itself diagonally leaks, so keep corners tight.
  • If a loop encloses nothing, the fence just harvests as a normal 3+ chain. Then columns cascade and refill.

Why it is ours: It reinterprets the same drag gesture from "a line you collect" to "a boundary you enclose" — a territory/area puzzle (think Qix/Splix) expressed entirely through our existing same-family chain. Hugely different feel, zero new input modality.
