01Introduction & Game Overview

Puzzle Craft 2 is a puzzle town-building game and the sequel to the original Puzzle Craft.

Mine, craft, build!

Match tiles, collect valuable resources, craft tools and build up your towns.

Influence & magical runes

Extend seasons, find new species, summon villagers, and more.

Land, air and sea

Discover new plants, animals and resources. Venture out to sea for supplies and long-lost treasures.

Rule them all!

Travel a massive kingdom, rule many settlements, and develop a mighty empire — the biggest Puzzle Craft yet.

Gold Stars on a tile indicate Bonus Experience is awarded when it is collected. Bonus XP for special puzzles scales with the number of friends in your Embassy (Friends List), plus extra from finishing rare collections. Example: a 100% bonus on a base 5 XP puzzle rewards 10 XP; a 200% bonus on the same puzzle rewards 15 XP.

05The Six Villages of the Kingdom

Travel across the kingdom, ruling each settlement in turn. Boards grow larger and hazards multiply as you progress.

1 · The Valley Settlement

Starting location, top-left of the kingdom map.

Farm: 5×5 board · 16 moves · 10 Coin entry.

Flowers & mounts unavailable. Long chains of fruits & cattle yield coins.

2 · The Hills Settlement

Introduces underground exploration.

Farm: 5×6 board · 10 moves · 30 Coin entry.

Mine: Introduces dirt, stone, and iron layers.

3 · The Northern Settlement

Known for its long winters.

Farm: 6×5 board · 10 moves · 70 Coin entry.

Winter is twice as long as any other season.

4 · The Coastal Settlement

Seafaring and large farms unlocked here.

Farm: 6×6 board · 16 moves · 100 Coin entry.

Sea: First seafaring board. Max moves: 17 navigation + 17 ship.

5 · The Swampy Bay Settlement

Swamp hazards are introduced here.

Farm: 6×6 board · 16 moves · 150 Coin entry.

Swamp hazards spawn from Spring to Autumn.

6 · The Island Settlement

Features a unique resource exchange system.

Farm: 6×6 board · 16 moves · 200 Coin entry.

Mine: None — trade resources at the Port for minerals.

07Trophies & Achievements

Earn trophies for feats of farming, seafaring, and exploration.

Bronze Trophies

  • Gather 36 chickens in one move.
  • Build 4 villages.
  • Rescue 200 puzzles from the swamp.
  • Ride the wave (click the beach umbrella decoration).

Silver Trophies

  • Complete a field, mine, or sea collection.
  • Gather 1000 manna.
  • Defeat the Pirate Boss.

08Tips, Tricks & Advanced Strategies

Squeeze maximum runes, gold, and bulk resources out of the board.

1 · Darin Kaplan’s Rune Farming Strategy

Runes are vital for purchasing magical species. The loop: use a Sapling tool to fill the board with trees, then a Golden Apple portal tool to transform trees into Lemons. Trace Lemons for coins, then coins for runes. Use Bird Feed to turn residual grass into Chameleons and repeat.

2 · Gold Farming Method

In the 4th village, configure Hen, Peccary, and Alpaca. Trace 36 hens → 36 peccary → 36 alpaca → 18 coins. This yields ~900 gold per chain.

3 · Bulk Resource Grinding

Dirt (7,500)

Use the 2nd village. Alternate using Drill and Shovel.

Salt & Water

Use the 6th village. Maximize ship moves and run the ship around the outer border.

09Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find fish in the sea?

Fish are earned by connecting Deep Water tiles. Once a fish tile spawns, move your ship over it to collect it.

Is there a PC version of Puzzle Craft 2?

Yes — available on Windows 8.1/10/11 through the Microsoft Store.

10Glossary

  • Navigation Move — Dragging a path to connect matching tiles on farm, mine, or sea boards.
  • Ship Move — Explicitly directing your ship’s movement on seafaring boards.
  • Runes — Rare currency used to unlock premium crops and Magic Portal tools.
